Outreach

Selected Presentations

Healthy Soils Workshop: Solano Resource Conservation District (24:15)

March 2020

I gave a talk at the Solano RCD Healthy Soils Workshop on "Healthy Soils, Healthy Microbes", focusing on how microbes and carbon storage are affected by practices such as reforestation and hedgerow planting.

Washington SoilCon 2020 - Soil Health: Not just for the top 1%

January 2020

At the Washington SoilCon 2020, I won an award for "Best Student Flash Talk" based on my research into mechanisms underlying subsoil carbon storage in long term agricultural systems.

TEDx Belmopan: What the Soil can Teach Us

September 2017

I gave a TEDx talk in Belmopan, Belize on the links between soil microbiology, carbon storage, and land development. Photos were provided by Tony Rath.

Solano RCD - Cover Crops and Water Movement

April 2022

I gave a talk at the Solano RCD Workshop on "Cover Crops and Water Movement", focusing on how cover crops impact water movement and storage in soils.
